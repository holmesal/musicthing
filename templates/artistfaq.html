Frequently Asked Questions

q

What do you get access to when I connect my SoundCloud account?

a

When you connect your SoundCloud account, we pull your artist photo and a list of your tracks, from which we ask you to choose one to be featured. We only store your chosen track and disregard the others. We pull genre information for that track, as well as city information for your account (that's so people can filter by city). We don't get access to any personal information - including your email (though if you would like to receive tips and information on building your following, you can subscribe from your "Manage" page).
